Getting the Lighting right:

Sun (Directional Light) - Static

Source Soft Angle = 10
Indirect Lighting Intensity = 2
Light Source Angle = 10
Shadow Exponent = 5

SkyLight - Static

Cubemap resolution = 64 (can be set higher if reflections turn purple-ish)
Sky Distance Threshold = 1
Intensity Scale = 4(depends on the map, as this is how much light the sky casts on the world)
Light Color = white(change it if you want for stylistic reasons)

Reflection Captures

Drag at least one onto the map

Use Sphere Reflection Captures for exterior spaces

Use Box Reflection Captures for interior spaces

Reflection Captures make reflections more accurate within the space they cover
